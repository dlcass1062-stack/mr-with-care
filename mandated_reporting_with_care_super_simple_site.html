Mandated Reporting 101 (Very Short)

What is it?

Some jobs require you by law to report suspected abuse or neglect of a child. This duty is called mandated reporting.

Why do it?

To help keep kids safe. Morally, we care for others (Gilligan). We follow fair rules and duties (Kohlberg). We notice, judge, and act (Rest).

How to think

  • Use objective notes (what you saw/heard).
  • Avoid leading questions.
  • Follow policy and timelines.
  • Be kind and trauma‚Äëinformed.
